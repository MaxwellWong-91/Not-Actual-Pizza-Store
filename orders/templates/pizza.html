Regular Pizza
Our Regular pizza is a thin-crust or Neapolitan pizza. They come in 2 sizes: small (12" in diameter) and large (16" in diameter). The small is suitable for 1 to 2 people while the large feeds 2 to 4 people.
Sicilian Pizza
Our Sicilian pizza is a deep-dish or thick-crust pizza. We sell them in a small 9-slice (16" x 16") or a large 15-slice size (28" x 16"). The small feeds 3 to 5 people while the large feeds 6 to 9 people.
